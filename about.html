Our Story

Sable & Strand Hair Studio was founded in 2018 by creative director Maya Chen, who envisioned a salon where clients could escape the ordinary and embrace their authentic selves. What began as a small two-chair studio has grown into one of Riverdale's most beloved hair destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: exceptional hair care starts with genuine connection. We take time to understand your lifestyle, preferences, and hair goals before ever picking up our scissors. This consultative approach ensures that every client leaves not just looking great, but feeling truly understood.

We are committed to ongoing education, with our team regularly attending workshops and training sessions to stay current with the latest techniques and trends. At Sable & Strand, you'll find the perfect blend of timeless expertise and contemporary innovation.
